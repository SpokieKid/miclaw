EinClaw Product Overview (Hardware & Software)

Business Overview

The Company specializes in the research, development, and operation of AI products for consumers. Revenue is generated primarily through hardware sales and software subscriptions. Website: https://www.einclaw.com

Product Lines

1. EinClaw Mic (Hardware)

A portable smart microphone built on the ESP32-S3 chip with a built-in 4G cellular module. Users press a button to record, audio is uploaded to the cloud via 4G, processed by AI, and returned to the user. The product will launch via a crowdfunding platform and be sold to consumers globally.

2. OpenClaw (Software Subscription)

A personal AI Agent hosting platform for individual users, built on Docker containerization. Subscription fee: USD 50 / month, collected through Lemon Squeezy, WeChat Pay, Alipay, and other payment channels.

3. Einko (iOS App)

An AI-powered screenshot management application, monetized via App Store subscriptions.

Role of the Singapore Entity

The Singapore entity serves as the Company's international business hub, responsible for receiving overseas investment, collecting overseas sales revenue, and signing cross-border partnership agreements.
